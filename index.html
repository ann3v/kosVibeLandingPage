About KosVibe

One mobile platform for discovering the best of Kosovo.

KosVibe was created to simplify exploration. Instead of jumping between scattered websites, users can discover places, events, and useful travel information through one clean, reliable, and user-focused application.

Our mission

Make exploring Kosovo easier, faster, and more enjoyable.

KosVibe helps tourists plan memorable experiences while giving local residents a more convenient way to discover restaurants, attractions, events, and hidden gems across the country.

Why it was built

The project began as a university software engineering initiative with a clear goal: create a modern digital product that promotes tourism, improves accessibility to local information, and helps businesses become more visible.

Technology

Built with React Native and Expo, and supported by Supabase, KosVibe combines a fast mobile interface with dynamic content, authentication, and real-time data management.
